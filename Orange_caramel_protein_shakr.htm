Orange caramel protein shake

  Description

   This orange caramel protein shake is reminiscent of the famous ice cream bar.
   It's tasty, healthy, and sure to keep you full until lunch or beyond.

Benefits of protein shake.

Protein shakes can offer several benefits, primarily related to muscle growth and recovery, weight management, and overall health. They can help build and repair muscle tissue, especially after exercise. Additionally, they can aid in weight loss by promoting feelings of fullness and supporting a healthy metabolism. Protein shakes can also be a convenient way to increase protein intake, which is important for various bodily functions like hormone production, enzyme creation, and wound healing.

Ingredient

  • 1 orange - zested, peeled, and separated into segments
  • 1 cup soy milk
  • 1 (5.3 ounce) container plain Greek yogurt
  • 5 ice cubes
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  • Step 1: Gather all ingredients.

  • Step 2:Combine orange segments, zest, soy milk, yogurt, ice, protein powder, and vanilla in a blender

  • Step 3:Blend untile smooth
